SERMON BY PASTOR JOHN.

CHRIST IS THE LIGHT.

John 1:4-5. In Him was life, and the life was the light of men, and the light shineth in darkness and the darkness comprehended it not.

Christ is the light of the world. The bible says that that light shines in darkness and the darkness comprehended it not.

The Amplified version says that the darkness could not overcome it.

Child of God, Christ (the light of the world) dwells in you. That means that in you there is light; and the light cannot be overcome by any powers of curses, failure, premature death and any other darkness in the world.
