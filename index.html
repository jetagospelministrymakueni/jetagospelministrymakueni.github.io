ABOUT JETA GOSPEL MINISTRY

Our Profile.

Jesus End Time Awakening (JETA) gospel ministry, is a Grace Gospel focused Church. Our purpose is to preach the gospel of God's grace all over the world, with intent of bringing salvation to all human race and making disciples.

Tim2:3 Kjv. For this is good and acceptable in the sight of the God our saviour, who will have all men to be saved and come to the knowledge of the truth. Discipling saints as per Christ's command.

Math 28:19-20. Kjv. Go ye therefore, and teach all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, and of the son and of the Holy Ghost: 20. Teaching them to observe all things whatsoever I have commanded you: and lo, I am with you always, even unto the end of the world. Amen.

We are located about 200M from Wote town on your way to the Governor's office behind The Makueni School Academy.

OUR STATEMENT OF FAITH.

Our statement of faith is strongly in our vision and mission of our calling,thus enabling us to reach humanity of different Backgrounds Or Race By Preaching Of The Gospel Of our Lord Jesus Christ.
We Believe In God, The Father Almighty, Creator Of Heaven And Earth.
We Believe In Jesus Christ, His Only Son, His Deity, Who Was Conceived By The Holy Spirit And Born Of The Virgin Mary.
He Suffered Under Pontius Pilate, Was Crucified, Died, And Was Buried; He Descended To Hell.
The Third Day He Rose Again From The Dead. He Ascended To Heaven And Is Seated At the Right Hand Of God The Father Almighty.
We Believe In The Holy Spirit, The Communion Of Saints, The Forgiveness Of Sins, The Resurrection Of The Body, And The Life Everlasting. Amen.
We Believe The Bible To Be The Inspired, The Only Infallible, Authoritative Word Of God.
We Believe That For The Salvation Of Lost And Sinful Man, Regeneration Of The Holy Spirit Is Absolutely Essential By The Hearing Of The Preaching Of The Gospel.
We Believe In The Present Ministry Of The Holy Spirit By in whom believers are baptized into.
We Believe In The Resurrection And That We Shall Rise Again
We Believe In The Spiritual Unity Of Believers In christ.
We believe there are God's angels who are minister for saints.
We believe in not worshiping angels but accept their ministry unto us.
